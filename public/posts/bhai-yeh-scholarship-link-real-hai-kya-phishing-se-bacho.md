Arre yaar, dekh! Ek "Guaranteed Internship" ka email aaya hai, direct selection! Bas is link pe click karke details fill karni hai. Sound familiar? *Ruko zara, sabar karo!* Yeh ek digital jaal ho sakta hai jise kehte hain **Phishing**.

Toh yeh **Phishing** hai kya cheez? Simple bhasha mein, yeh ek online scam hai jahan hackers fake emails, messages, ya websites banakar aapki personal information, jaise passwords, bank details, ya OTPs, churane ki koshish karte hain. They might pretend to be your college admin, a bank, or even your favourite food delivery app. Unka goal hota hai aapko dara kar ya laalach dekar aapse aisi information nikalwana. Ek galat click aur aapka social media account hack ho sakta hai, ya *worse*, bank account khaali!

Yeh scammers *bahut* clever hote hain. Woh urgency create karte hain, jaise "Your account will be blocked in 24 hours!" taaki aap panic mein aa jao. But aapko smart rehna hai.

Toh isse bachein kaise? Simple hai, bas thoda alert rehna hai:
*   **Sender ki jaanch karo:** Email ID ko dhyaan se dekho. `info@amazon-deals.in` aur `info@amazon.in` mein zameen-aasman ka fark hai.
*   **Hover before you click:** Link pe click karne se pehle, uspar mouse le jaao. Screen ke bottom-left corner mein asli website ka URL dikhega. Agar woh ajeeb sa lage, toh *bilkul* click mat karna.
*   **Spelling aur Grammar mistakes:** Bade companies usually aisi silly mistakes nahi karti. Agar email mein errors hain, toh woh ek *major* red flag hai.

> Pro Tip: Golden rule yaad rakho - *'Shak hai, toh click mat kar'*. Agar koi offer itna accha lag raha hai ki sach nahi ho sakta, toh woh shayad sach nahi hai. Apni personal details online share karne se pehle hamesha double-check karo.

So agli baar jab koi "exclusive discount" ka link aaye, thoda sa detective ban jao. Stay smart, stay safe online

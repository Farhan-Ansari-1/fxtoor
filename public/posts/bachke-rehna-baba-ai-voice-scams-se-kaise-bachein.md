Arre dost, imagine karo – you get a call. Awaaz ekdum tumhare best friend jaisi hai, tension mein bol raha hai, "Yaar, emergency hai, jaldi se online paise transfer kar de!" Aap kar bhi dete ho, but later you find out it was a scam. Welcome to the world of **AI Voice Cloning**, scammers ka naya hathiyar!

Yeh koi sci-fi movie ka scene nahi hai, *it's happening for real*. Scammers ko bas aapki ya aapke friend ki awaaz ka ek chhota sa sample chahiye hota hai – jo unhe social media videos ya Instagram Reels se aaram se mil jaata hai. Phir, using **Deepfake Audio** technology, woh uss awaaz mein kuch bhi bulwa sakte hain. Scary, hai na? Pehle photo edit hote the, ab awaaz bhi copy ho rahi hai.

Toh is naye digital jhol se kaise bachein? Don't worry, aapka tech dost hai na! Here are some simple tips:

*   **Double-check Karo:** Agar koi aisi urgent call aaye, toh phone kaat kar uss person ko unke saved number par waapis call karo. *Always verify before you transfer.*
*   **Personal Sawaal Poochho:** Ek aisa secret question poochho jiska jawab sirf aapka asli dost ya family member hi de sakta hai. Jaise, "Apne college fest ka naam kya tha?"
*   **Urgency se Saavdhan:** Scammers hamesha urgency aur panic create karte hain taaki aap sochne ka time na lein. *Take a deep breath and think logically.*

> Pro Tip: Apne close friends aur family ke saath ek secret 'code word' set karlo. Agar kabhi aisi suspicious call aaye, toh woh code word poochho. No code word, no money transfer. Simple!

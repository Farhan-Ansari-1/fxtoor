Pehle waale **Phishing** emails ko pakadna aasan tha. Spelling mistakes, ajeeb formatting... pata chal jaata tha ki kuch gadbad hai. But ab AI ki wajah se, yeh scam emails *bilkul perfect* hote hain. They can use your name, your college's name, and create a sense of urgency that feels very real. Aisa lagega jaise genuine company se hi mail aaya ho! Yeh targeted attacks, jinhe **Spear Phishing** kehte hain, bahut dangerous ho sakte hain. Ek galat click aur aapka personal data ya social media account *gayab*!

Toh tension mat lo, apne tech dost ke paas solution hai. Bas thoda alert rehna hai.

*   **Sender Check Karo:** Hamesha sender ka email address double-check karo. `info@placement-amazon.com` is not the same as `info@amazon.com`.
*   **Links pe Hover Karo:** Kisi bhi link par click karne se pehle, uspar mouse cursor le jaakar dekho ki woh aapko *asli mein* kahan le jaa raha hai.
*   **Pressure Mein Mat Aao:** Scammers "urgent action required" jaisi lines use karte hain. Relax, take a deep breath, and think before you click.

> **Pro Tip:** Agar aapko kisi professor ya dost se koi urgent email aaye jismein paise ya personal info maangi gayi ho, *hamesha* unhe call ya WhatsApp karke confirm karo. Email par aankh band karke bharosa mat karna.

AI smart hai, but hum usse bhi zyada smart ban sakte hain. Stay safe, stay aware, and share this with your friends. Apne college group mein bhi daal dena!

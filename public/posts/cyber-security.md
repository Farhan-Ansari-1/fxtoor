Aaj ke digital yug mein cybersecurity ek bahut hi important topic ban gaya hai. Har jagah internet ka use badh raha hai, aur isi ke saath cyber attacks bhi badh gaye hain.

Cybersecurity ke Basic Points:
Data Protection: Aapka personal data, jaise passwords aur banking details, secure rehna chahiye.
Phishing Attacks se Bachav: Fake emails aur websites se saavdhan rahen.
Strong Passwords: Unique aur complex passwords use karein.
Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Extra security layer lagayein.
Cybersecurity sirf bade corporations ke liye nahi hai, balki har individual user ke liye zaruri hai. Agar hum apne devices aur data ko surakshit nahi rakhenge, to hackers aasani se access kar sakte hain.

Aapko apne accounts aur systems ko regularly update karna chahiye aur suspicious activities par nazar rakhni chahiye.

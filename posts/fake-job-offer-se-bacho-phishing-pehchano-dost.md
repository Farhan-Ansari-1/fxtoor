College life mein sabse bada tension kya hota hai? *Placements!* Aur imagine karo, ek din aapke inbox mein ek dream company se internship offer aaye. Excitement level 100! But wait, thoda ruko. Yeh asli hai ya... koi scam ka chakkar?

Aaj kal **Scammers** bahut smart ho gaye hain, yaar. Woh **Phishing** attacks use karke students ko target kar rahe hain. Phishing ka funda simple hai – ek fake email ya message bhejkar aapki **Personal Details** (jaise password, bank details, ya Aadhaar number) chori karna. Yeh fake job offers bilkul asli lagte hain!

Toh is fraud ko kaise pehchanein? *Bahut aasan hai*, bas in cheezon par dhyan do. Agar email mein spelling mistakes ya poor grammar hai, toh woh ek red flag hai. Real companies professional hoti hain. Agar koi aapse interview se pehle hi paisa maang raha hai—chahe woh "registration fee" ho ya "security deposit"—toh *seedha block kardo*. Real companies job dene ke liye paisa nahi leti hain.

*   **Check the Sender's Email:** Kya email ID `hr.amazon@gmail.com` jaisi lag rahi hai? Yeh fake hai. Official companies hamesha apne domain (e.g., `@amazon.com`) se email karti hain.
*   **Suspicious Links:** Email mein diye gaye link par cursor le jao (click mat karna!). Link ka URL check karo. Agar woh ajeeb ya unofficial lag raha hai, toh *bilkul click mat karna*.
*   **Too Good to Be True:** Agar offer itna acha hai ki vishwaas nahi ho raha, jaise bina interview ke high salary, toh woh shayad sach nahi hai.

> Pro Tip: Koi bhi offer accept karne se pehle, uss company ki official website ya LinkedIn page par jaakar check karo. Wahan "Careers" section mein dekho ki woh job listing hai ya nahi. Direct company ko contact karke bhi verify kar sakte ho!

Toh dosto, online thoda savdhaan raho. Har chamakti cheez sona nahi hoti. Apne dosto ke saath bhi yeh info share karo. Stay smart, stay safe
